Practical Catering Tips Most Couples Miss

These make a real difference to your event

Avoid too many fried starters

Guests feel full early & skip the main course

Limit paneer dishes to 2

Too much paneer feels repetitive — variety matters

Keep at least 40% menu regional

Guests always remember authentic Rajasthani flavors

Use shaded serving zones

Improves food quality & guest comfort outdoors

Always include hydration drinks

ORS-style sharbat — especially for daytime weddings

Add a live counter

Dosa, chaat or grill — keeps guests engaged & excited
